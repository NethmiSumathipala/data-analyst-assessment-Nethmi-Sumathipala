#Identify the trend in sales over time
#Extract month and year from 'date' column
data["month"] = data["date"].dt.to_period('M')

#Group by 'month' and sum the total sales
monthly_sales = data.groupby("month")["total_sales"].sum().reset_index()

#Plot the monthly sales trend
plt.figure(figsize=(10, 6))
plt.plot(monthly_sales["month"].astype(str), monthly_sales["total_sales"], marker="o",color="darkorchid")
plt.title("Monthly Sales Trend")
plt.xlabel("Month")
plt.ylabel("Total Sales")
plt.grid(True)
plt.tight_layout()
plt.show()

Talk about monthly sales trend April 2023 reached 120, the highest in this dataset, suggesting a peak in sales during this month. The total sales for May 2023 decreased to 75, showing a significant decline from the previous month.
